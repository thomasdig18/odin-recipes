Banana Banana Bread

Description

This delicious baked bread is perfect for seasons turning. With 7 ingredients
you will have your family asking what that amazing smell is as they walk in
the door. Perfect for family gatherings or a late night treat, you won't get better
than this.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan

  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together
    butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana
    mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.

  3. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the
    loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.

  4. Enjoy!
